More About The Research Paper

  • Novel Task and Dataset – 6,000 Reddit posts (2,000 real inspiring, 2,000 non-inspiring, 2,000 LLM-generated inspiring) balanced across India and the UK.

  • AI vs. Human Language Markers – Syntactic & lexical differences in LLM-generated vs. real inspirational posts, across cultures.

  • Inspiration Detection Insights – Assessment of performance across diverse data sources (real vs. fake) and cultures.
