Hint to ace in your game

  • •
    Analytic writing: LLMs love structure. There’s more logic and less personal touch.
  • •
    Descriptiveness – LLM post overflow with adjectives. They’re more descriptive and elaborate, painting detailed scene. Like “Radiant sunrise of infinite hope.”
  • •
    Readability – Human developed post are easier to read, they keep it heart felt. LLM ones use longer words and more complex phrasing by keeping it highbrow.
